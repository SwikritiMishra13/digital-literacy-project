Stay Safe Online Checklist:

Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks
Do not approve payment requests without checking
Use strong passwords for all accounts
Enable two-factor authentication
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
Regularly check bank transaction history

UPI-Specific Tips:

Do not accept collect requests from unknown people
Always verify UPI ID before sending money

Reporting:

Report fraud at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline number: 1930
